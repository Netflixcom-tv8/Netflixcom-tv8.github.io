Netflix is a popular streaming platform that provides a wide range of movies, TV shows, and original content for its subscribers to enjoy. These shows and movies are available to stream on a variety of gadgets, such as phones, tablets, TVs, computers, game consoles, and set-top boxes, through internet connectivity.


Netflix provides a monthly subscription service. You can stream Netflix through multiple gadgets at the same time, depending on the plan you choose.




How to create a Netflix account?


To create a Netflix account, you can follow these steps:
  1. On your computer or mobile device, open a web browser.
  2. Visit the Netflix.com/tv8 website.
  3. Click the "Join for free for one month" or "Sign up" button. Specific wording may differ slightly.
  4. Choose a plan that best suits your priorities. Netflix offers different subscription plans with varying features and prices. Select the one that best suits your needs by clicking on the respective "View Plan" or "Continue" buttons.
  5. Enter your email address and create a password. Be sure to use a valid email address that you have access to, because Netflix will send you a verification email and important account information to this address. Create a strong password that combines letters, numbers, and symbols.
  6. Click on the "Continue" or "Next" button.
  7. Choose your method of payment. Netflix accepts various payment options, including credit cards, debit cards, PayPal, and gift cards. Provide the required information based on the payment method you have chosen.
  8. Please enter your billing information. Provide the necessary details, like your name, billing address, and payment details.
  9. Please review and verify your account. Check that all the information you have provided is accurate, and review the Terms of Use and Privacy Policy. Click on the "Start Subscription" or "Join Netflix" button to proceed.
  10. Please confirm your email ID. Netflix will send a verification email to the address you provided during the sign-up process. Open the email and click the verification link to confirm your account.
  11. Set up your profile. Once your account has been verified, you can personalise your Netflix profile by choosing a profile name and adding a profile picture.
  12. Start enjoying Netflix. Now you can browse and stream a wide variety of TV shows, movies, documentaries, and more on Netflix.
